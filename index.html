Our Story

Shaping the Future of Somalia

Founded as a focal point for Somali development, Jobkey University strives to be among the leading universities in the Horn of Africa — recognized for academic excellence, student success, and research that drives sustainable growth.

Our graduates are working across the business sector, local and international NGOs, contributing to health, economics, communications, education, and technology sectors nationwide.

"Students don't care how much you know until they know how much you care." — John C. Maxwell. This is the spirit Jobkey University teaches by.

🔑 Admissions

How to Unlock Your Seat

Three simple steps to join Jobkey University. We review every application personally.

01

Fill The Application Form

Complete the online application with your personal and academic details. No special formula — we review the whole person.

02

Submit Your Documents

Gather: Secondary Certificate, Transcripts, Passport Photo, Recommendation Letter. Submit via email or in person.

03

Receive Your Decision

The Admission Committee reviews and notifies you. Application fee is $35 (waivers available for financial hardship).

Education Shouldn't Cost a Dream

Jobkey believes every Somali deserves access to higher education. We offer three scholarship pathways.

Institutional

Jobkey's Own Scholarships
Awarded to students from vulnerable communities through partnerships with local NGOs and school principals. Full and partial awards available.
